Overview

John invites readers to slow down, notice the movement of the text, and respond to God's character with trust, obedience, and worship. This page is designed as a launching point for personal study, group discussion, podcast planning, and video teaching.

Key Themes

  1. Signs reveal Jesus' identity and invite belief.
  2. Jesus' teachings center on life, light, truth, and love.
  3. The Gospel calls readers into abiding faith and witness.

Suggested Study Path

  1. Study the signs and 'I am' statements across the Gospel.
  2. Trace themes of belief, testimony, and eternal life.
  3. Reflect on the farewell discourse and resurrection appearances.
