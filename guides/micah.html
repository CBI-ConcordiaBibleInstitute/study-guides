Overview

Micah invites readers to slow down, notice the movement of the text, and respond to God's character with trust, obedience, and worship. This page is designed as a launching point for personal study, group discussion, and sermon preparation.

Key Themes

  1. God confronts corruption among leaders and people.
  2. True worship is expressed through justice, mercy, and humility.
  3. Hope centers on God's future shepherd-king and restored people.

Suggested Study Path

  1. Read Micah's alternating sections of warning and hope.
  2. Memorize and discuss Micah 6:8 within the book's wider message.
  3. Trace promises of Bethlehem and restored leadership.
