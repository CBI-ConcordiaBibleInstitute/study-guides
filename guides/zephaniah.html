Overview

Zephaniah invites readers to slow down, notice the movement of the text, and respond to God's character with trust, obedience, and worship. This page is designed as a launching point for personal study, group discussion, podcast planning, and video teaching.

Key Themes

  1. God calls His people to repentance before judgment falls.
  2. The nations and Judah alike stand under divine scrutiny.
  3. Restoration ends in singing, joy, and renewed presence.

Suggested Study Path

  1. Read the warnings connected to the day of the Lord.
  2. Notice how judgment clears the way for renewal.
  3. Celebrate the closing vision of God rejoicing over His people.
