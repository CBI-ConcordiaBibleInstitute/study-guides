Overview

Romans invites readers to slow down, notice the movement of the text, and respond to God's character with trust, obedience, and worship. This page is designed as a launching point for personal study, group discussion, podcast planning, and video teaching.

Key Themes

  1. The gospel reveals God's righteousness for Jews and Gentiles alike.
  2. Justification by faith reshapes identity and hope.
  3. Transformed living flows from God's mercy and grace.

Suggested Study Path

  1. Read Romans 1–4 for Paul's explanation of the gospel.
  2. Study Romans 5–8 on grace, union with Christ, and life in the Spirit.
  3. Apply Romans 12–15 to worship, community, and mission.
