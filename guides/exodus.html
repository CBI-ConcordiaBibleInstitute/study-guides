Overview

Exodus invites readers to slow down, notice the movement of the text, and respond to God's character with trust, obedience, and worship. This page is designed as a launching point for personal study, group discussion, podcast planning, and video teaching.

Key Themes

  1. God rescues His people from slavery.
  2. The covenant shapes worship, holiness, and community life.
  3. The tabernacle reveals God's desire to dwell with His people.

Suggested Study Path

  1. Follow Israel's rescue from Egypt in Exodus 1–15.
  2. Study the covenant and law in Exodus 19–24.
  3. Consider the tabernacle and God's presence in Exodus 25–40.
