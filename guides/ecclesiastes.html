Overview

Ecclesiastes invites readers to slow down, notice the movement of the text, and respond to God's character with trust, obedience, and worship. This page is designed as a launching point for personal study, group discussion, and sermon preparation.

Key Themes

  1. Life's fleeting nature pushes readers toward deeper wisdom.
  2. Human striving alone cannot secure ultimate meaning.
  3. Reverence for God grounds faithful living amid uncertainty.

Suggested Study Path

  1. Notice the repeated refrain about life's vapor-like nature.
  2. Discuss work, pleasure, time, and mortality across the book.
  3. Summarize how the conclusion reframes the search for meaning.
