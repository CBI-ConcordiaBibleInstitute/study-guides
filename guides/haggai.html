Overview

Haggai invites readers to slow down, notice the movement of the text, and respond to God's character with trust, obedience, and worship. This page is designed as a launching point for personal study, group discussion, podcast planning, and video teaching.

Key Themes

  1. God calls His people to reorder their priorities.
  2. Obedience matters in seasons of rebuilding.
  3. Present faithfulness is tied to future hope and glory.

Suggested Study Path

  1. Observe the prophetic messages tied to specific historical moments.
  2. Discuss how apathy toward God's house revealed deeper issues.
  3. Connect Haggai's encouragement to perseverance in ministry today.
